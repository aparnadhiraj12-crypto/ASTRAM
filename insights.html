BMTC buses are the #1 breakdown source

Among all vehicle breakdowns, BMTC buses account for the largest share — more than double the next category. Targeted fleet maintenance could meaningfully cut breakdown-driven congestion.

1,466 of 4,896 breakdown events
🕘

Evenings drive the most incidents

21:00 is the single busiest hour for traffic events, with the 19:00–22:00 window accounting for a disproportionate share of all incidents — likely tied to evening commute volume.

810 events logged at 21:00 alone
🏚️

Potholes have the longest tail

While potholes are rarely "high severity" individually, their median time-to-resolution massively outpaces every other cause — pointing to a maintenance backlog rather than an emergency response gap.

Median 12,990 min to close (~9 days)
